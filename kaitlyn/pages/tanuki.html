Tanuki

Tanukis are a omnivorous species from Japan also known as raccoon dogs, due to their facial resemblance to raccoons. They have brown or grey fur and a fluffy dark-colored tail. Tanukis are found in forest areas and usually close to water. Their diet consists of small animals, fruits, berries and plants. Tanukis are known for their roles in japanese folklore as mischevious and masters of disguise.
