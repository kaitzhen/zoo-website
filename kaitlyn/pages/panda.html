Giant Panda

The giant panda, classified by its black and white fur, is a bear indigenous to China. On average, they are about 4-5 feet and weight approximately 300 pounds. Pandas are often found in forests where they feed on bamboo, which makes up almost their entire diet. They can eat up to 30lb of bamboo daily Pandas were labeled as an endangered species for a long time, but they were recently updated to "vulnerable" species.
